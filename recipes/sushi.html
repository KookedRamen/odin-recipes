Salmon Sashimi

Description

A delicious recipe for the most tasty of appetizers. Best served with fresh wasabi, ginger, soy sauce, or a delicious side of soy yuzo citrus ponzu.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Grade Salmon
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger
  • Soy Yuzo Citrus Ponzu

Steps

  1. Sharpen knife: To ensure a clean and beautiful cut, sharpen your knife or purchase a pre-sharpened sushi knife.
  2. Slice the Salmon: Cut the salmon into 1/4 inch thick (or your preference) pieces.
  3. Garnish: garnish with your favorite sides. Popular sides are soy sauce and pickled ginger.
