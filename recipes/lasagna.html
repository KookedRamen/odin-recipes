Lasagna

Description

Red sauce lasagna with cheese and meat. This Recipes is the most terrific recipe for Lasagna you will ever make. Not only will you make this lasagna, you will make it again and agian for years to come.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Lasagna
  • Mozzarella
  • Parsley
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parmesan
  • Parsley
  • Beef and Sausage
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water
  2. Meat Sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer and Bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with the lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly
