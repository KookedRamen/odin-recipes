Charred Chile–Marinated Grilled Chicken Tacos

Description

To help build deep layers of flavor in these tacos, Fermín Núñez begins his marinade by toasting and charring aromatics: dried chiles, cinnamon, allspice, and onions all hit the heat before soaking with juicy chicken thighs for an hour, imparting smoky flavor before they cook quickly on the grill or in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 6 Cloves of garlic
  • 5 dried guajillo chiles
  • 3 medium-size dried chiles de árbol
  • 4 whole allspice
  • 2 small white or yellow onions (about 1 pound), cut into 1-inch wedges with root ends left intact
  • 1 (1-inch) cinnamon stick
  • 1 ½ tablespoons kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ cup water

Steps

  1. Make the marinade: cook the chiles, garlic, and onions. Add salt and pepper. Allow to cool.
  2. Marinade the chicken: completely submerge chicken with your marinade for 1-5 hours
  3. Cook the chicken: remove the chicken from the marinade letting excess drip off. Cook chicken on grill until 165 degrees F flipping every 6 minutes
  4. Shred and enjoy: shred the chicken and enjoy on some warmed up corn tortillas.
